THE LIVESEY ARMS

Fresh British food from the field & the shore to the kitchen & the bar

With a long history of revitalising customers and providing
respite for weary travallers and local alike(1792),under the
direction of Rosie Dicker,we are proud to be the next chapter in
the story of The Livesey Arms.

Having undergone a total revamp lifting this ancient into the
21st century,renovations have included a state-of-the-art kitchen,
more dining space and open fires for snug autumn evenings

ROSIE DICKER

Known throughout her lincolnshire for her top quality food

A career dating back to the 70's establishing an enviable record of
distinction in restaurant management from her days as a student
of Cordon Bleu with Linn Park at the Carafe in Market Rasem,
The Angelin Wainfleet and Alfreds in Louth

Rosie prides herself in sourcing the best ingrediants cooked simply
to create modern British food
